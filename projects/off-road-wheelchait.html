Off-Road Wheelchait

Description

This project focused on a human-centric off-road wheelchair platform balancing ergonomics, robustness, and manufacturability. I led mechanical design and kinematic trade-off analysis for a lightweight frame intended for demanding terrain.

The design used bicycle-principle hand levers to improve torque transfer while maintaining controllability, with structural decisions validated against load targets and real-use constraints.

Technologies Used

  • SolidWorks (CAD)
  • Kinematic and ergonomic analysis
  • Aluminium / carbon-fiber structural design
  • Prototype-oriented mechanical engineering
