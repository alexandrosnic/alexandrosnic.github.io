ML Projects

Description

My ML work spans supervised learning baselines and deep-learning pipelines for perception and autonomous behavior. I use ML as a deployable engineering component, with emphasis on data quality, robustness, and transfer to real operating conditions.

Representative work includes behavioral cloning for autonomous driving, where CNN models were trained on camera streams with augmentation-heavy preprocessing to improve recovery behavior and generalization to unseen trajectories.
