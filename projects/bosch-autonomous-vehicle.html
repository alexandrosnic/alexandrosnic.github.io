Bosch Autonomous Vehicle

Description

During my career at Bosch, I worked on the Level 4 autonomous driving project aimed at delivering safe self-driving cars on public roads. My responsibilities included developing unit tests in C++ and CUDA, researching and optimizing transport protocols (ETH, UDP, RDMA), and contributing to container setups (Docker, ROS 1&2) for both local and embedded devices.

Technologies Used

  • C++ & CUDA
  • Docker
  • ROS 1&2
  • RDMA, ETH, UDP
  • Wireshark, VTune, perf
