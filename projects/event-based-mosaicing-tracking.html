Simultaneous Mosaicing and Tracking with Event Cameras

High-temporal-resolution vision pipelines for dynamic scenes

Description

This work investigates event-based camera processing for simultaneous visual tracking and mosaicing, targeting low-latency perception in fast-motion environments.

Capabilities Demonstrated

  • Developed asynchronous vision logic for event-stream data.
  • Combined tracking and map-building into one real-time pipeline.
  • Evaluated robustness under rapid motion and sparse visual cues.

Technologies Used

  • Computer Vision
  • Event-Based Cameras
  • Tracking and Mosaicing
  • Python / C++
