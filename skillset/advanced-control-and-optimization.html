Advanced Control & Optimization

Description

Precision in robotics is a product of robust control logic. I specialize in designing control systems that handle complex dynamics. My work involves deriving the mathematical models of mechanical systems and implementing State-Space Control laws to ensure stability and minimize oscillations. I focus on optimizing the trade-off between response speed and precision, ensuring that sensitive hardware can maintain high-accuracy positioning even under dynamic loads.

Key Achievement

LQR-Based Antenna Stabilization: I developed a full control stack in Matlab/Simulink for a satellite antenna system. By implementing a Linear Quadratic Regulator (LQR), I optimized the control gains to achieve zero-steady-state error and rapid damping of vibrations during high-speed telescopic extensions. This ensured the antenna could maintain a lock on satellite signals with minimal power consumption and mechanical wear.
