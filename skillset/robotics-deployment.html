Robotics Deployment & Field Engineering

Description

Great code can still fail in a dirty construction site or a high-traffic urban road. I act as the 'bridge' between R&D and the customer. I spent two months on-site in Singapore (and then more back in Germany for Conbotics) deploying a wall-painting robot, where I was responsible for diagnostic monitoring, recording rosbags for edge-case debugging, and identifying mission-critical bugs. I developed the minimum viable case studies needed to certify the robot's efficiency for the end customer, effectively acting as both a deployment engineer and a technical consultant

Key Achievement

Successfully managed the international deployment of a prototype robot in Singapore, identifying and delegating fixes for critical Sim2Real gaps that only appeared in real-world environmental conditions. My work contributed in securing a follow-up contract for Conbotics with the same client, and reaching the market earlier than anticipated.
