Embodied AI, AI Agents & ML

Description

My expertise in AI spans from classical Machine Learning (SVMs, HMMs) to modern Transformer architectures and Large Language Models (LLMs). I don’t just use LLMs as chatbots; I build Autonomous AI Agents capable of tool-use and reasoning. To validate my work, I developed and benchmarked a general-purpose AI agent against the GAIA benchmark on Hugging Face, achieving high-ranking performance in complex, multi-step task completion. My Computer Vision experience complements this, utilizing CNNs and YOLO-based perception to feed real-world spatial data into neural reasoning engines.

Key Achievement

Behavioral Cloning for Autonomous Driving. I engineered a complete end-to-end deep learning pipeline for an autonomous vehicle. By leveraging Behavioral Cloning, I trained a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) to map raw pixel data from front-facing cameras directly to steering commands. This project involved extensive data augmentation and preprocessing (OpenCV) to ensure the model could generalize to unseen tracks, effectively teaching a machine to 'drive' by mimicking human intuition rather than following hard-coded rules.
