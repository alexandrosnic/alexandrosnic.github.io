Perception: Image-Based & Event-Based Computer Vision

I build perception pipelines from classical image-based CV to event-based vision, depending on scene dynamics and latency constraints.

I implement robust detection and semantic mapping workflows (for signs, humans, structural elements, and custom classes) that integrate into autonomy stacks.

I design asynchronous event-camera pipelines with reconstruction, optical-flow estimation, and mosaicing/tracking for high-motion environments.
