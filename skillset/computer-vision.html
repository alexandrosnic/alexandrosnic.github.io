Perception: Image-Based & Event-Based Computer Vision

Description

I work with vision systems that see beyond traditional frames. My experience ranges from standard Image-Based CV (training YOLO on traffic signs and lane detection) to cutting-edge Event-Based CV. Using neuromorphic data, I’ve implemented image reconstruction via leaky integrators and optimized optical flow estimation. I’ve even built simultaneous mosaicing and tracking systems for rotating event cameras, turning raw asynchronous events into high-fidelity panoramic maps.

Key Achievement

Developed real-time vision systems with semantic mapping that detect objects such as traffic signs, doors, windows, humans or whatever the customer wants.
